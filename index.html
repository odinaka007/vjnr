Standard Curriculum

Kindergarten

Preschool educational approach based on playing, singing, practical activities such as drawing, and social interaction as part of the transition from home to school.

Nursery School

Nursery School is for children aged three to about five before their entering mainstream education or primary school. It is the starting point for a childs development and the key foundation of the Nigerian Educational System.

Primary

Elementary education runs from grade one through six (ages 6-12). Students are awarded the Primary School Leaving Certificate on completion of Grade 6, based on continuous assessment.

About Us

We are a school which offers continuity of education and development in a happy, safe, friendly and culturally responsive community where self-confidence, self-esteem and respect flourish.

We develop the whole essence of the child, encompassing the brain, the brawn and the beauty, working from inside all the way through to the outside. We foster genuine love for learning through carefully planned and enriched school experiences.
